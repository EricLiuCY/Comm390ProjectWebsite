Bench Accounting

Bench Accounting was founded in 2012 as 10 Sheet Inc. by several UBC alumnis. Bench is dedicated to offering smart bookkeeping solutions to small and medium sized businesses in Canada. Unlike traditional bookkeeping platforms, Bench utilizes a smart digital platform that users can use to log, track, and analyze their own financials.
