EcoCloud

This is EcoCloud, a decentralized datahub
that tracks operations waste.

  • Tell Me More

To help companies realize that sustainability
is a benefit for them and our Earth. By creating
a software that seamlessly connects business
operations to sustainability efforts, we aim to
improve the environment we live in.


EcoCloud is a subscription based service that allows businesses to track their operations waste
and make better decisions through the insights our applications provides.

Manage your operations carbon footprint, energy consumption, and more with our cloud platform.

EcoCloud provides its partners with an application for their customers to see the environmental impact of their purchases.
